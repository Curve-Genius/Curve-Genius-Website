    Our Mission

    Curve Genius helps teachers and students create fair academic environments. Our curving calculator uses statistics that are easy to understand. We're committed to helping you find the best configuration. Please visit our feedback page to share your experience.

    Our Challenge

    Our founder, Varun Rodrigues, created Curve Genius to fix the problem of traditional grading curves. These curves unfairly punished hard-working students and often shamed high scoring students, creating a negative learning environment for all.

    Our Story

    Varun sought to understand traditional grading methods and found they lacked in mathematical motivation. He worked with teachers to create a website that could help them address grading issues and promote statistics education.
